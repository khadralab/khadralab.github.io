Principal Investigator

Professor Anmar Khadra

Full Professor, Department of Physiology

Co-Director, Centre for Applied Mathematics in Bioscience and Medicine

Professor Khadra's research focuses on developing mathematical and computational models to understand complex biological systems. He applies techniques from nonlinear dynamics, stochastic processes, and computational methods to study problems in neuroscience, immunology, and cell biology.
