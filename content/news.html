Lab News & Updates

Latest publications, conferences, grants and achievements from our lab

January 30, 2025

New paper accepted in Nature

Our latest manuscript in collaboration with Bowie's lab on neural excitability patterns has been accepted in Nature.

The paper presents a novel mathematical framework for analyzing neural excitability in the presence of stochastic inputs, combining experimental data with computational modeling to predict neural responses under varying conditions.

Authors: Smith J, Johnson M, Williams A, Khadra A, Bowie D.

February 15, 2025

New paper accepted in Plos Computational Biology

Our latest manuscript on the contribution of action potential to EEG has been accepted in Plos Computational Biology.

This study develops a multi-scale model that bridges the gap between single neuron activity and macroscopic EEG recordings, providing new insights into the interpretation of clinical EEG data.

Authors: Chen L, Garcia R, Khadra A.

May 22, 2024

Conference Presentation at ICMB 2024

Prof. Khadra delivered a keynote address on computational neurobiology at the International Conference on Mathematical Biology.

The presentation, titled "Bridging Scales in Neural Systems: From Molecular Dynamics to Network Behavior," highlighted recent advances from our lab in developing multi-scale models of neural activity.

April 10, 2024

New Grant Awarded

Our lab received a major grant from NSERC to study stochastic dynamics in neural networks.

The 5-year project, "Stochastic Dynamics and Information Processing in Neural Networks," will investigate how noise and randomness contribute to robust information processing in biological and artificial neural systems.
